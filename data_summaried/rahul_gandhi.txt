Rahul Gandhi (About this soundpronunciation (help·info) [ˈraːɦʊl ˈɡaːn̪d̪ʱi]) (born 19 June 1970) is an Indian politician and a member of the Indian Parliament, representing the constituency of Wayanad, Kerala in the 17th Lok Sabha. He is also a trustee of Rajiv Gandhi Foundation and Rajiv Gandhi Charitable Trust.
